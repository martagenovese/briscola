Rules of Briscola

Game Overview

Briscola is a trick-taking card game played with a 40-card Italian deck.

Card Values

  • Ace (Asso): 11 points
  • Three (Tre): 10 points
  • King (Re): 4 points
  • Horse (Cavallo): 3 points
  • Jack (Fante): 2 points
  • All other cards: 0 points

How to Play

  • Each player receives 3 cards
  • One card is turned face up to determine the trump suit (Briscola)
  • Players take turns playing one card each
  • The player who plays the highest card of the led suit wins the trick
  • Trump suit (Briscola) beats any card of other suits
  • Winner of each trick leads the next one

Winning

The game ends when all cards are played. The player with the most points wins.
